Import & Export

End-to-end international trade facilitation — from documentation to delivery.

International Trade

Navigating Global Trade for Nigerian Businesses

International trade carries layers of complexity that many businesses are not equipped to manage alone — customs documentation, port procedures, regulatory compliance, foreign supplier coordination, and domestic delivery all have to work in concert for a simple import to arrive without delay or penalty. Mcmoren Multiservices exists to absorb that complexity on behalf of Nigerian businesses, managing every step of the import or export journey from origin documentation to final delivery in-country.

Our team brings practical expertise in Nigerian Customs regulations, NPA procedures, and international freight forwarding, combined with an internationally minded approach to supplier and agent relationships across multiple trade corridors. Whether you are importing industrial machinery from Europe, sourcing consumer goods from Asia, or exporting Nigerian products to regional markets, we provide a single-point solution that keeps your goods moving and your business compliant.

Nigerian Customs expertise — NCS, NPA, SON, NAFDAC compliance
End-to-end facilitation from foreign supplier to Nigerian delivery
Multiple trade corridors — Europe, Asia, West Africa, Americas
Full documentation management, zero compliance exposure
What We Offer

Our Trade Services

Comprehensive import and export facilitation — every document, every step, every border.

Freight Forwarding

We arrange international freight by sea or air on your behalf, selecting carriers, booking space, and coordinating bill of lading or airway bill documentation. Our agent network across key trade routes ensures your cargo gets competitive rates and reliable scheduling from origin to Nigerian port.

Get a Freight Quote

Customs Clearance

We handle the full customs clearance process at Nigerian ports — preparing and filing declarations, managing duty assessment, liaising with NCS officers, and coordinating examination and release. Our clearance team knows the procedures, the timelines, and the right people to engage to keep your cargo moving without unnecessary delays.

Discuss Clearance

Import Documentation

Importing into Nigeria requires a precise set of documents — Form M, Combined Certificate of Value and Origin, Pre-Arrival Assessment Report, and agency-specific approvals from bodies such as SON, NAFDAC, and NESREA where applicable. We prepare, submit, and track all required documentation so you are never held up by paperwork.

Get Documentation Support

Export Documentation

We manage the full documentation set for Nigerian export shipments, including export licences, standards compliance certificates, phytosanitary certificates where required, and commercial documentation for the receiving country. Our export documentation service ensures your goods clear Nigerian ports efficiently and arrive at destination without regulatory complications.

Enquire About Exports

Supply Chain Coordination

We coordinate the full inbound supply chain for import clients — from engagement with foreign suppliers and shipping lines through port clearance and domestic delivery. Vendor management, shipment tracking, and timeline coordination are handled by our team so your internal resources remain focused on running your business, not managing logistics.

Discuss Supply Chain

Trade Advisory

International trade rules, tariff classifications, and agency requirements change — and getting it wrong is expensive. Our trade advisory support helps businesses entering new import or export markets understand their regulatory obligations, choose the right shipping terms, and structure transactions to minimise duty exposure and compliance risk.

Request Trade Advice

Every shipment we manage is held to full regulatory standard — not because we have to, but because that's how we protect your business."
