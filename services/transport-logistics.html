Transport & Logistics Operations

Our Transport and Logistics operations are supported by a modern fleet of trucks, buses, cars, and other essential equipment — ensuring seamless service and exceptional customer satisfaction. Operating at major Nigerian ports, we provide efficient conveyance of machinery, containerised goods, and general cargoes to a wide range of destinations.

Our passenger logistics services are tailored to meet diverse class requirements, delivering comfort, reliability, and convenience. In addition, we offer specialised equipment including cranes and cargo-handling machinery, available upon customer request to support various logistics operations efficiently and safely.

Our Capabilities

What We Operate

Cargo Haulage
Nationwide road freight for containerised goods, machinery, and general cargo. Our truck fleet handles port pickup through inland delivery.
Port Operations
Efficient conveyance operations at major Nigerian ports — coordinating container collection, discharge, and dispatch from port to client destination.
Passenger Logistics
Fleet of buses and cars for passenger transport services — tailored to class requirements with a focus on comfort, reliability, and scheduling precision.
Crane & Heavy Equipment
Specialised cranes and cargo-handling machinery available on request — for industrial lifting, off-loading, and heavy cargo operations.
Contract & Project Logistics
Dedicated fleet arrangements for long-term logistics partnerships — account managed, volume-priced, and SLA-backed for consistent, reliable service.
Fleet Support & Coordination
Fleet scheduling, maintenance coordination, and driver management ensuring operational reliability and compliance with Nigerian road transport standards.
