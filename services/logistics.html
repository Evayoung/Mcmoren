Logistics & Haulage

Reliable road freight and cargo haulage solutions across Nigeria.

Our Capability

Nationwide Freight You Can Depend On

Mcmoren Multiservices operates a robust road freight and haulage network covering all commercial routes within Nigeria, from Lagos and Abuja to Port Harcourt, Kano, and beyond. We manage diverse cargo categories — dry goods, industrial materials, agricultural produce, and equipment — with a focus on safe handling, on-time performance, and end-to-end coordination that keeps your supply chain moving.

Our logistics division is built around a core principle: your cargo is your business, and your business cannot afford delays. We combine experienced drivers, well-maintained vehicles, responsive dispatch, and thorough documentation to deliver outcomes that our clients can plan around with confidence — from the first booking to the final waybill.

Nationwide Coverage — all major commercial routes
Diverse Fleet Capacity — from small consignments to heavy loads
Real-Time Coordination — responsive dispatch and transit updates
Full Documentation — waybills, delivery confirmation, compliance records
What We Offer

Our Logistics Services

A comprehensive range of freight and haulage capabilities to support businesses at every stage of their supply chain.

Road Freight & Haulage

We move goods of all sizes across Nigerian roads with a fleet configured for both short-haul and long-haul operations. Cargo is secured to industry standards, and our drivers are trained for safe transit on all route types — urban corridors, highways, and rural access roads.

Last-Mile Delivery

The final mile is often the hardest to get right. We handle direct-to-recipient delivery within cities and commercial zones, ensuring your goods arrive at the exact destination — properly signed for and documented, not just dropped at a gateway.

Request a Quote

Fleet & Contract Logistics

For businesses with recurring freight needs, we offer dedicated fleet arrangements and contract logistics agreements. This gives you predictable capacity, consistent pricing, and an assigned team that understands your cargo profile and delivery requirements over time.

Enquire About Contracts

Cargo Handling & Supervision

We provide cargo handling, loading, and offloading supervision to ensure that goods are managed correctly at each transfer point. Fragile cargo, oversized loads, and sensitive materials receive appropriately trained handling teams and equipment throughout the process.

Documentation & Waybills

Every consignment we handle is supported by complete, accurate documentation — waybills, delivery receipts, cargo manifests, and any regulatory filings required for the route. Our documentation process is thorough so your records are always complete and audit-ready.
