Division 02 — NCS Licensed · Trade Compliance

Expert Customs & Freight Services
for Nigerian Businesses

McMoren MultiServices Limited, licensed by the Nigeria Customs Service, offers comprehensive customs brokerage and freight forwarding services — ensuring the smooth, compliant, and timely movement of goods across borders.

Our team of experienced professionals navigates complex customs regulations, efficiently handling documentation, duties, and clearance processes to eliminate delays for customers' import and export cargoes. We coordinate freight forwarding for all types of cargo — containerised goods, machinery, and general cargo — leveraging reliable transport partners to deliver both domestically and internationally.

"We navigate Nigerian Customs (NCS), the Nigerian Ports Authority (NPA), Standards Organisation of Nigeria (SON), NAFDAC, and international trade compliance so you don't have to. Every shipment we manage is held to full regulatory standard — because that's how we protect your business."
What We Handle

Our Customs & Freight Services

Customs clearance at all Nigerian ports of entry
Form M processing and pre-arrival assessment (PAAR)
Duty assessment and payment coordination
Import and export documentation management
International freight forwarding — sea and air
Bill of lading and airway bill coordination
SON, NAFDAC, NESREA agency approvals
Export clearance and phytosanitary certificates
Trade advisory and tariff classification
Supply chain coordination from origin to delivery
