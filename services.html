Division 02

Customs Brokerage & Freight Forwarding

International trade into and out of Nigeria carries layers of regulatory complexity — from Form M filings and pre-arrival assessments to coordinating with SON, NAFDAC, and the Nigerian Customs Service. Mcmoren provides expert customs brokerage and freight forwarding services that ensure your goods clear without delay, penalty, or compliance exposure.

Customs Clearance

End-to-end customs clearance at Nigerian ports of entry. We prepare and file declarations, manage duty assessment, liaise with NCS officers, and coordinate physical examination and cargo release promptly and correctly.

Start Clearance

International Freight Forwarding

We arrange sea and air freight on your behalf — booking space with carriers, managing bill of lading or airway bill documentation, and coordinating the movement of goods across key trade corridors including Asia, Europe, and the Americas.

Get Freight Quote

Form M & Import Documentation

Complete management of all Form M processing, Combined Certificate of Value and Origin, PAAR, and agency-specific approvals for regulated goods. We prepare, submit, and track all documentation so your import never stalls on paperwork.

Get Documentation Help

Export Documentation

We manage the full documentation set for Nigerian exports — licences, standards compliance certificates, phytosanitary certificates, and commercial documentation for receiving countries, ensuring your exports clear Nigerian ports efficiently.

Enquire About Exports

Trade Advisory

Expert advisory for businesses entering new import or export markets — tariff classification, regulatory obligations, shipping terms, and duty minimisation strategies. We help you trade smarter and stay protected from compliance risk.

Request Advisory
